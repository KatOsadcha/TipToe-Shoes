About Us

TipToe Soles is an Australian owned and operated company that has been designing and manufacturing comfortable footwear for over 20 years. We are passionate about creating shoes that are both stylish and functional, and that will keep your feet happy all day long.

We believe that everyone deserves to have access to high-quality, affordable footwear, and that's why we offer a wide range of shoes to suit every budget and lifestyle. Whether you're looking for a comfortable pair of sneakers for everyday wear, or a stylish pair of heels for a special occasion, we have the perfect shoes for you.

At TipToe Soles, we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible shopping experience. That's why we offer a variety of convenient features, such as free shipping on orders over $60, a generous exchange and returns policy, and friendly customer service.

We are also proud to be a sustainable company. We use high-quality materials and construction methods to ensure that our shoes last, and we are constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental impact.
